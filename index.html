What is Masala Pasta?

Italian Pasta meets the Indian Masala to create a delicious & amazing Indo-Italian dish. It was first introduced on the streets of India and has been an all time favorite for families.

~Ingredients

  • Penne
  • Macoroni
  • Fusilli
  • Rigatoni
  • Carrots
  • Bell Peppers
  • Green Peas
  • Sweet Corn
  • Mozarella
  • Hot water

Spices

  • Salt
  • Garam Masala
  • Coriander Powder
  • Kashmiri Chilli Powder
  • Tumeric

~Preparation

  1. Boil the pasta and cook on medium flame for 6-8 minutes
  2. Drain the water rom the cooked Pasta

Make Masala for Pasta

  1. Heat oil & Butter in a pan
  2. Add chopped onions and fry till golden brown
  3. Add chopped vegetable and fry for 2-4 minutes
  4. Stir in the salt and spices
  5. Add tomatoes and tomato paste
  6. Mix and cook on medium to medium-high heat ntil masala smells good

Masala + Pasta

  1. Add cooked pasta into masala paste and mix well
  2. Grate mozarella or any cheese of choice
  3. Cover and cook on low heat
  4. Sprinkle coriander leaves and serve masala pasta hot
