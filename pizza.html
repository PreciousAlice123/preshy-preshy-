ABOUT MILAN PIZZA

It is one famous pizza born in the city of Milano,Italy. Maybe the least known among typical Italian pizza styles, the Milan-style pizza is a “newer”version created in the 1950s. It is thick and fluffy,like focaccia, even though it only needs to rise for one hour.

How to make milan pizza

~Ingredients

  • PLain Flour
  • Self-Raising Flour
  • Salt
  • Dry Mustard
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Butter
  • Egg
  • Milk

Filling

  • Olive Oil
  • Finemy Minced Meat
  • Coarsely Chopped Tomatoes
  • Anchovies
  • Finely Chopped Onions
  • 1 Garlic Clove
  • Celery
  • Persley
  • Grated Cheese of choice

Method of Preparation

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. In a bowl,sift the flours,salt,mustard and cayenne
  3. In a differnt bowl,beat the eggs and milk together
  4. Stir the mixtures together
  5. Knead the dough on a floured surface then press the dough into a pizza plate
  6. Saute the minced meat in oil then let cook
  7. In a bowl,combine the minced meat,cheese,tomatoes,anchovies,onion,garlic, celery,and persley

Meat + Dough

  1. Spread meat mixture evenly over dough
  2. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 - 25 minutes or until crust is brown
